Mandatory Documents to Claim Category Concessions

For OBC / EWS / VJNT / SBC:
  • Caste Certificate & Caste Validity Certificate (except EWS)
  • Non-Creamy Layer (NCL) Certificate valid up to March 31, 2026
  • Income Certificate issued by Tahsildar (Family income ≤ ₹8 Lakhs)
  • EWS eligibility certificate (for EWS quota)
For SC / ST Candidates:
  • Caste Certificate issued by competent authority in Maharashtra
  • Caste Validity Certificate
  • Income Certificate issued by Tahsildar (Family income ≤ ₹2.5 Lakhs for full GOI Scholarship)
  • Freeship eligible up to ₹8 Lakhs family income
Disclaimer on Management Quota:

Please note that all fee concessions and government scholarship schemes are strictly applicable **only for admissions secured through CAP rounds**. Management Quota / Institutional Quota admissions are required to pay 100% full fees and are not eligible for government scholarship refunds.
