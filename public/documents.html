DTE Regulations

  • Receipts Allowed: DTE permits uploading Cast Validity/NCL receipts during initial registration, but original certificates **must be produced** at the reporting center (ARC) to confirm the admission.
  • NCL Expiry Date: Non-Creamy Layer certificates must be valid up to **March 31, 2026**. Older validity ranges will be rejected.
  • Income Limits: State scholarship concessions require Income Certificate issued by Tahsildar for family income **≤ ₹8 Lakhs**.
